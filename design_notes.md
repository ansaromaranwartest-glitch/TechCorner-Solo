# TechCorner Reference Website Design Notes

## Color Scheme
- Primary Blue: #3B82F6 (bright blue for header gradient and buttons)
- Secondary: Coral/Red accent (#F87171 for icons/badges)
- Background: White/Light gray
- Text: Dark gray/Black

## Header Navigation
- Logo on left
- Navigation: Jobs, Tasks, Tenders, AI Hub, For Employer, Pricing
- Right side: Language selector (EN), Login, Sign Up buttons
- Sign Up button has coral/red background

## Hero Section
- Gradient blue background
- "AI-powered matchmaking" badge
- "Find Your Dream Job" headline
- Subtext about opportunities
- Search bar with job search input and location selector
- "Search Jobs" button

## Main Content Layout
- Left sidebar with filters (Parent Categories, Categories, Job Type, Remote Work, Sort by Date)
- Main content area showing job listings
- Job cards with company logo, title, location, job type, date, deadline
- Summary section for each job
- "View" button to see full details
- Pagination at top right
